The legend of PicoHERO2

The princess in the fairyland was abducted by the dragon. Can you knock down the dragon to save the princess!

How to play

You can move the PicoHERO with up down left and right button. When the PicoHERO touches the enemy, the fight start. If you win the fight, you can get experience points.

If life is 0, game is over.
You can continue to play, if you choose continue at title menu.
