The tower of SHADOW

The shadow appeared again. The shadow is in top of the tower. Can you knock down the shadow?

How to play

You can move the hero with up down lef and right button. To attack a monster, touch A or B button.
If you flick the screen, you can change the hero’s direction.

You can move the blue box cursor on the item icons at the top right of the screen. If you touch the blue box, you can use the item.
